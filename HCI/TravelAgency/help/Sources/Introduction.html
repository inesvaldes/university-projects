OpenWorld Agency - Application overview

OpenWorld Agency is a travel application that allows you to book theme park tickets and associated accommodations in a simple and intuitive way. The application is designed for use at point-of-sale terminals in travel agency branches.

Main features

  • View all theme parks: See detailed information about each park, including description, location, and prices.
  • View all accommodations: Filter accommodations by type and category and see details of each one.
  • Random special offer: Each time the application starts, one theme park is chosen randomly and all tickets and accommodations associated with that park have a 15% discount.
  • Flexible booking order: You can start by booking a park or an accommodation; both paths are valid.
  • Editable reservation: Modify or remove parts of your reservation before final confirmation.
  • Customer information: Enter the customer details required to formalise the booking.
  • Summary and confirmation: Review all booking details before saving them to a file.
  • Mini game: After confirming the booking, play a small game that may award a 100â‚¬ voucher for the next booking.

Basic workflow

  1. Start at the Main Menu.
  2. Choose to begin with Theme Parks or Accommodations.
  3. Add one or both: tickets and/or accommodations for the same theme park.
  4. Review and modify your reservation in the Reservation panel.
  5. Enter your personal details in the Customer Information panel.
  6. Check the final details in the Reservation Summary panel.
  7. Confirm the booking, save the file, and optionally win a voucher in the Mini Game.
